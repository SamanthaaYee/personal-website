ABOUT

Hi! My name is Samantha Yee and I'm in my second year at the University of Waterloo studying Systems Design Engineering!

“What even is Systems Design Engineering?” - a question I asked myself when applying.

To me, SYDE allows me to explore two key ideas: systems that help us learn about the world, and design that allows us to change it. This combination drives me to approach problems with a holistic view, always seeking creative and practical ways to improve user experiences through thoughtful, well-designed solutions.

I'm currently working as a Web Developer at Apricotton and I've had three previous co-ops as a UI/UX Designer and a Software Engineer.

I’m always seeking the perfect mix of innovation, curiosity, and a little fun in my work. Feel free to reach out - I’d love to connect!
